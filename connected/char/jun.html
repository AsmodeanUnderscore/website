CONNECTED // CHARACTERS
JUN
NameJun Knight
GenderFemale
Age26
RaceHuman
FactionHCPD
AppearanceJun’s a very short woman, standing at a measly 5’ 4”, with a leaner build. Her skin is ghostly pale, deeply contrasting her dark brown eyes. Her black hair has pre-maturely began to grey in streaks, and is always kept up in a high bun.
PersonalityA woman of few words by choice, she never says anything more than what needs to be said. She often comes off as a reserved daydreamer, when Jun is mostly just observant. Jun always prefers the facts in a situation, usually listing out evidence in her life and in crime scenes based on how likely they are to be true or fabricated. She’s competitive to a fault, always striving to be better. Because of this internal competitiveness, she will often forsake sleep just to work on cases. However, she’s able to keep a consistent energy level when sleep deprived out of sheer force of will, which is taking a toll on her health. She has an strong sense in what she thinks is right and wrong, sometimes acting upon her moral compass without a second thought.
SkillsJun is an intelligent individual, specifically when if comes to chemistry. Her observational skills are also very high, and has great social awareness she pretends she doesn’t have.
BiographyJun got her Associate Degree in Forensic Science quickly after college, and got herself a job as a forensics investigator for the Hack City Police Department with hopes of becoming a detective. While she knows it’s unlikely because of the superiority of Replicas in the field, she works tirelessly to eventually reach her dreams. She currently has had multiple successful cases, clawing her way slowly to being at the forefront of fighting crime.
