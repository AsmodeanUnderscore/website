NameJames Starwalker

AppearanceStanding at 5’ 10”, James tends not to stand out very much. He has a well toned, lean muscle build. James has bright red hair and piercing blue eyes, he wears his hair tied back in a short ponytail, has a well groomed goatee and usually wears an old, dark cloak to hide his hair and swords.
PersonalityJames is secretive, untrusting and usually wary of strangers. Once you know him, he is generally a nice guy. James tends to keep to himself. He hates his link to the Network. He also tends to try and talk his way out of fighting, if at all possible.
SkillsBrilliant swordsman, extremely adaptable to situations, persuasive, and is quite competent at disguise.
BiographyBorn near the borders of Tasra and Maryn, James learned how to wield a sword and use his link to the Network from a young age. Everything he knows, he learned from his parents. They taught James everything from swordplay to hunting, even manipulation through speech. Upon turning nineteen, James was sent to go live out in the world. He was to learn what people were like and how to hide among them. The reason for this is because his parents were “vigilante hackers”, wanted dead or alive by Connecticorp. So as a result, James chose to hide in the most obvious place… Hack City. Here he is able to hide anywhere and pretty much be anyone. While living in Hack City, he was discovered by the gangs and mobsters of the city and even now they are trying to recruit him.
