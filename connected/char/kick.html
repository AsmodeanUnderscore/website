CONNECTED // CHARACTERS
DANIELLE
NameKiqura ("Kick")
GenderMale
Age28
RaceHorned
FactionNone (previously Black Masks)
AppearanceStanding a somewhat impressive 6'3", Kiqura has the typical markers of any Horned: pale white skin, a three foot long spiked tail and two curved horns. All of these, however, are hidden behind a layer of minor shapeshifting which is only dropped in private, or if a tail would be particularly useful. In public, he retains his medium length white hair, short goatee and vermilion eyes (which are definitely contact lenses, believe me), but darker skin and a small Black Masks tattoo, which he displays as little as possible.
PersonalityKiqura's main motive is profit - his name does mean "bank vault", after all. For him, success in life can be measured by availability of options - that you have the option to turn down a job in favour of another, for example, instead of having to take anything offered because there's no other choice, that you can leave the White Zone and set up a semi-successful life instead of being stuck in banditry. Money is the quickest way to expand options, and the quickest way to get Kick to do what you want him to do. There's little he won't stoop to doing for a little bit of quick cash.
SkillsKick's time spent in the White Zone has honed his survival skills to a sharp and ruthlessly efficient tip. If pressed into a situation where his life is under threat, he'll stop at nothing to turn the tables. He is persuasive, charismatic and a good liar; you won't survive long in the White Zone - or in Hack City proper for that matter - if you're being honest with everyone. He's competent with computers and small arms, mostly a product of his brief tenure with the Black Masks. He has three spare stashes of cash, totalling roughly $5000 each, just in case.
BiographyKiqura was born in 179, and made plans to leave the White Zone shortly after. He worked his way through the Black Masks as a teenager, doing a generally shady things until he had enough money to vaguely get by and obtain a fake ID. As of 207, he has obtained a degree in management and business studies and has made a moderate amount of money trading on the stock exchange. Although officially Kick has cut all ties with the Black Masks, they still occasionally text him, offerring quick little jobs for some quick little cash.
