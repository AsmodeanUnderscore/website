CONNECTED // CHARACTERS
DANIELLE
NameDanielle
GenderFemale
Age12
RaceReplica
FactionHCPD
AppearanceStanding at 6’2 with a toned but athletic body build & fair skin complexion, Danielle has very androgynous features with very few scars or tattoos to show. She has blue eyes & silver hair which she keeps in an long bob or in a ponytail when the situation calls for it. Her body is a mix of robotic and synthetic flesh, her face, head & neck looks & feels like most human skin however her entire body is robotic or cybernetic in aesthetic.
PersonalityOften speaking when something needs to be said, Danielle tends to keep quiet but she remains a polite and professional individual with some eccentric mannerisms when it comes to organisation & social interactions such as conversations with herself out loud. She also has a few habits such as flicking the lid of a zippo lighter she carries with her for sentimental value, even sometimes lighting it when idle or in deep thought.
SkillsDanielle is not only programmed with hand to hand combat & marksmanship knowledge along with Law Enforcement tactics. She’s also designed as an investigator with knowledge in psychology, deduction & criminology. she’s also a natural when it comes to computers and hacking.
BiographyCommissioned 12 years ago for the Hack City Investigation Department of the HCPD, Danielle was designed to be both a highly skilled police officer & detective in one neat package, often working behind the scenes when developing a case for the DA to use against any criminals the HCPD arrest. Whilst her reputation precedes her as a no nonsense copper and excellent detective, she finds herself at odds with ConnectiCorp and their employees and questions if she could be more than what she is programmed & designed to be.
